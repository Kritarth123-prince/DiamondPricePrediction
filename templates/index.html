Welcome to the Diamond Price Prediction System!

This system uses machine learning to predict diamond prices based on the 4 C's and physical dimensions:

  • Carat: Weight of the diamond
  • Cut: Quality of the cut (Fair, Good, Very Good, Premium, Ideal)
  • Color: Color grade (D to J, where D is colorless)
  • Clarity: Clarity grade (I1 to IF)
  • Dimensions: Length (x), Width (y), Depth (z) in mm
